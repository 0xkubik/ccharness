Invoke the `roadmap-management` skill (the grounding loop) with this scope:

> $ARGUMENTS

roadmap-management is the harness's **grounding front door**. On a fresh repo it captures the product's
**North Star** (vision · core problem · level) at the top of `.claude/ccharness/roadmap.md`, then
**offers** to lay out the **roadmap** — a **flat, ordered list of features** below it, built top to
bottom, from where the product is now to that North Star. Other product skills (`what-to-do`, `how-to-do`, `do`,
`musician`) route here when no North Star exists. Re-run any time to revise — the goal or the roadmap;
roadmap-management reads what already exists and adapts, no flag needed.
