Invoke the `crux` skill (the diagnosis loop) with this pain/doubt/blockage:

> $ARGUMENTS

crux **pins the real problem** from your input + the repo (autonomously — it interrogates the
problem, not you), runs a **four-lens panel** over it — Jung's Sensation (facts) / Intuition
(pattern & reframe) / Thinking (logic) / Feeling (worth) — where every lens must commit to a
falsifiable check or be muted, then **converges on ONE diagnosis + angle of attack**, not a
checklist and not implementation. It is free-standing (no North Star needed) and the deliberate,
deeper cousin of `/slap`. By default it stops at the diagnosis; it can hand the angle to
`/how-to-do` or `/do` if you want to build on it.
